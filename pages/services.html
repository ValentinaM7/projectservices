Perfecting the hunt

Details of the product

3 Piece versatility Our BOONDI Roller polespear 9 ft - is comprised of 3 modular carbon fibre sections. Each section is approximately 91cm (3ft) long- meaning that wherever your standard long blade fins go, you can take your polespear with you! All 3 sections including injector rod, slip tip, rubber and rigging weigh an impressive 1.2kg!

Highest quality raw materials As Castrol states “oils ain’t Oils” the same goes for Carbon fibre… Strenuous workshop and infield testing has proven its worth when it comes to our carbon fibre sections- You can rest assure All BOONDI rollers are constructed from Japanese Toray T300 fibre- the world leaders in carbon fibre technology. The same goes for all stainless steel components, using duplex 2205 grade stainless means greater strength and corrosion resistance- Making each section absolutely Marinised and up for any punishment.

Unrivalled Slip tip design Our Custom Patent Pending ‘Grip Tip’ features the following: - a razor sharp tri cut point, meaning it cuts through flesh and bone on penetration unlike a conventional pencil point. - 8mm - 630 grade hardened stainless steel injector rod and slip tip= straighter shots and high impact resistance. - unlike other slip tips on the market that regularly fall off or require constant cord tension to keep it in place- Our Grip tip utilises simple yet incredibly effective engagement between injector rod and tip that ensures your tip is in place and dead straight until disengagement is required.

Rigged to perform - Powered by world leaders in rubber tech, USA made primeline rubber propels our mighty BOONDI roller polespear. All additional rigging is highest quality European cordage all splicing and crimping is all done inhouse by us.
